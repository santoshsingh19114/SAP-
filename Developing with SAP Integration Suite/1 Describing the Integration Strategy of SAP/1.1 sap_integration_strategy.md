# Presenting the SAP Integration Strategy

## **What Is SAP Integration?**
SAP Integration refers to the seamless connection of SAP and non-SAP systems, applications, data sources, and business processes using standardized tools, APIs, and integration patterns. Its purpose is to ensure consistent, real-time data flow across the enterprise, enable end-to-end business automation, and support scalable, flexible, and intelligent operations. SAP Integration makes it possible for organizations to unify fragmented systems, improve process efficiency, enhance data accuracy, and accelerate innovation through a connected digital landscape.

# **3. The Role of SAP Business Technology Platform (SAP BTP)**
SAP BTP is the core foundation of SAP’s integration strategy.

### **Key Capabilities of SAP BTP:**
- Unified, open, business-centric cloud platform
- Integrates applications, business processes, and data
- Supports AI-powered business processes
- Accelerates innovation and time-to-value

SAP BTP powers Cloud ERP modules such as:
- Spend Management
- Supply Chain
- Human Capital Management
- Customer Relationship Management

# **4. SAP Integration Suite**
A major component of SAP BTP, enabling powerful integrations.

### **Capabilities:**
- Integrate SAP and non-SAP applications
- Enable real-time data flow
- Provide tools for complex integration scenarios
- Support event-driven architectures

Helps organizations achieve end-to-end automation, consistency, and operational agility.
